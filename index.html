Tic Tac Toe

1. Turn-based Play: Players take turns placing their marker (X or O) on an empty cell.

2. Winning Condition: The first player to align three markers horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.

3. Draw Condition: If all cells are filled and no player has aligned three markers, the game ends in a draw.
